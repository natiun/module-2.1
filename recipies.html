Brownies

Instructions Ingredients
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan. 1/2 cup butter
2. In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan. 1 cup white sugar
3. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook. 2 eggs
4. To Make Frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Chicken Noodle Soup

Instructions Ingredients
1.Pat chicken dry with paper towels; sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon pepper and salt. In a 6-qt. stockpot, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken in batches, skin side down; cook until dark golden brown, 3-4 minutes. Remove chicken from pan; remove and discard skin. Discard drippings, reserving 2 tablespoons. 2-1/2 pounds bone-in chicken thighs
2.Add onion to drippings; cook and stir over medium-high heat until tender, 4-5 minutes. Add garlic; cook 1 minute longer. Add broth, stirring to loosen browned bits from pan. Bring to a boil. Return chicken to pan. Add celery, carrots, bay leaves and thyme. Reduce heat; simmer, covered, until chicken is tender, 25-30 minutes. 1-1/4 teaspoons pepper, divided
3.Transfer chicken to a plate. Remove soup from heat. Add noodles; let stand, covered, until noodles are tender, 20-22 minutes. 1/2 teaspoon salt
4.Meanwhile, when chicken is cool enough to handle, remove meat from bones; discard bones. Shred meat into bite-sized pieces. Return meat to stockpot. Stir in parsley and lemon juice. Adjust seasoning with salt and remaining 3/4 teaspoon pepper. Discard bay leaves. 1 tablespoon canola oil
5. 1 large onion, chopped
6. 1 garlic clove, minced
7. 10 cups chicken broth
8. 4 celery ribs, chopped
9. 4 medium carrots, chopped
10. 2 bay leaves
11. 1 teaspoon minced fresh thyme or 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
12. 3 cups uncooked kluski or other egg noodles (about 8 ounces)
13. 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
14. 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Scrambled Eggs

Instructions Ingredients
Beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper in medium bowl until blended. 4 eggs
Heat butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot. Pour in egg mixture. As eggs begin to set, gently pull the eggs across the pan with a spatula, forming large soft curds. 1/4 cup milk
Continue cooking – pulling, lifting and folding eggs – until thickened and no visible liquid egg remains. Do not stir constantly. Remove from heat. Serve immediately. 2 tsp. butter

Ramen

Instructions Ingredients
1.Cook the ramen noodles without the seasoning packet as directed on package. Drain and set aside. 2 3 Ounce Packages of Ramen Noodles
2.Heat the sesame oil in a small skillet or saucepan over medium heat. 2 Teaspoons Sesame Oil
3.Cook the garlic, stirring constantly for 2 minutes. Cook the garlic, stirring constantly for 2 minutes.
4.Remove the pan from the heat and whisk in the soy sauce, brown sugar, and sriracha until combined. 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
5.Toss the noodles with the sauce. 1 Teaspoon Brown Sugar
6.Garnish with green onions if desired. 2 teaspoons Sriracha

Peanut Butter & Jelly

Instructions Ingredients
Heat griddle or skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). 2 teaspoons butter
Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Spread peanut butter on unbuttered side of one slice of bread, and jelly on the other. Place one slice, buttered side down on the griddle. Top with other slice, so that peanut butter and jelly are in the middle. Cook for 4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown, and heated through. 2 slices white bread
1 teaspoon peanut butter
2 teaspoons any flavor fruit jelly
